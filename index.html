Frequently Asked Questions

What ERPs does Axal integrate with? +

Axal integrates with over 20 different ERPs including SAP, Oracle, QuickBooks, JobBOSS, Epicor, Infor, E2, Syspro, and many more. We can typically integrate with any ERP that has an API or import capability.

How long does implementation take? +

Most sites go live in about 1 week. We connect to your existing systems, learn your workflow, and start processing automatically - all faster than it takes to train a new hire.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes, our pricing is volume-based and per site with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime, and we'll right-size your plan if your volume changes.

What happens to orders with errors? +

Axal automatically flags missing or mismatched information and routes these exceptions for quick review by your team. About 60% of incoming POs are flagged before they hit your ERP, helping you catch mistakes before they cost you.

Do I lose control over my order processing? +

No, you maintain full control. Axal handles the legwork of data entry, validation, and acknowledgments, but you still control what gets sent and approved. Think of it as your best data entry agent, not a replacement for your team's expertise.
